Fuse Jam #01 Themes



At Fuse Jam each team has their own unique theme to spark ideas for coming up with a game. In Fuse Jam #01, the overall theme was "Emotions & Verbs". Each team had a combination of two words - one emotion and one verb - that they could use in any way they liked to inspire their ideas. To make the theme generating process more fun, we used a Bingo ball to give out numbers, which corresponded to the theme cards!
